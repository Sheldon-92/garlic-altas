The Multispecies Dance

The Non-Human Protagonist: Capsaicin & Synergy

Harissa is a pharmacological team-up. The garlic works synergistically with the chili to attack your pain receptors.

The Chemical Process: Capsaicin (from chili) activates the TRPV1 receptor (heat). Allicin (from garlic) activates both TRPA1 and TRPV1. The combination creates a multi-channel sensory activation more intense than either alone. The olive oil dissolves both compounds, making the burn linger longer on the tongue.

Cultural Context

Harissa is central to Tunisian identity (UNESCO Heritage). It represents the successful assimilation of a "New World" invader (the chili pepper from the Americas) into the "Old World" terroir of North Africa.
