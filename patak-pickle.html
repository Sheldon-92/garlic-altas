The Multispecies Dance

The Non-Human Protagonist: Lactobacillus & Mustard Oil

In this sauce, garlic enters a symbiotic pact with bacteria. By salting the garlic, humans create a brine where Lactobacillus thrives, converting sugars into lactic acid.

The Chemical Process: The mustard oil acts as a selective barrier‚Äîit suppresses mold (thanks to allyl isothiocyanate) while allowing the friendly lactic acid bacteria to ferment the garlic anaerobically. The result is a "cured" garlic where the flavor is mellowed and integrated into the spice blend.

Cultural Context

Achar (pickle) is the cornerstone of the Indian meal, providing high-intensity flavor contrast to rice or bread. It represents the agency of the sun and microbes in preserving the harvest for lean months. Patak's brought this ancient technique to the global supermarket.
